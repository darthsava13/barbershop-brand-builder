
# Crowd Pleezers Barber Shop — Single-Page Website

## Overview
A premium, edgy single-page scrolling website for Crowd Pleezers Barber Shop. Dark luxury aesthetic with black (#0A0A0A) background, gold (#D4AF37) accents, and white text. The uploaded logo will be embedded throughout the site.

## Design System
- **Background:** #0A0A0A (near-black)
- **Accent:** #D4AF37 (gold)
- **Text:** White primary, muted gray secondary
- **Headings:** Oswald (Google Fonts) — bold, uppercase
- **Body:** Inter (Google Fonts) — clean, modern
- **Feel:** Luxury streetwear brand — edgy, confident, premium

## Sections (in scroll order)

### 1. Sticky Navigation
- Transparent on top, solid dark background on scroll
- Logo on the left, nav links on the right
- Hamburger menu on mobile with slide-out drawer
- Smooth scroll to all sections

### 2. Hero
- Full-viewport section with dark gradient overlay on a background image placeholder
- Large bold tagline: **"Where Style Meets Culture."**
- Subtext: "Premium cuts. Bold style. Community first."
- Gold **"Book Now"** CTA button that smooth-scrolls to the booking section

### 3. About
- Two-column layout (stacked on mobile)
- Left: image placeholder
- Right: shop story in a confident, community-focused tone
- Gold accent line above the heading

### 4. Services & Pricing
- 3-column card grid (1 column on mobile)
- 7 services with prices: Classic Haircut $35, Skin Fade $40, Beard Trim $20, Hot Towel Shave $30, Kids Cut $25, Lineup $15, The Works $60
- Each card has a subtle icon, and a gold glow hover effect

### 5. The Barbers
- Horizontal card layout with 3-4 placeholder barbers
- Photo placeholder, name, title, short bio
- Gold **"Book with [Name]"** button on each card

### 6. Gallery — "Our Work"
- Masonry photo grid with 8-12 placeholder images
- Lightbox modal on click to view full-size

### 7. Testimonials
- Carousel with 5 placeholder reviews
- Gold star ratings, customer name, service received
- Dark card backgrounds with subtle gold border

### 8. Booking — "Book Your Appointment"
- Centered responsive iframe container (placeholder src for GoHighLevel link)
- Styled to match dark theme with rounded corners
- Note below: "Walk-ins welcome. Appointments recommended."

### 9. Find Us
- Two-column layout: Google Maps embed placeholder on the left, hours/address/contact on the right
- Click-to-call phone and click-to-email links

### 10. Footer
- Logo, social icons (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok) with gold hover
- Phone, email, copyright © 2026

## Animations & Interactions
- Fade-up on scroll for each section (using Intersection Observer)
- Parallax effect on the hero background image
- Floating gold **"Book Now"** button appears bottom-right after scrolling past the hero
- Gold glow hover effects on service cards
- Smooth scroll navigation

## Mobile Responsiveness
- Fully responsive across all breakpoints
- Hamburger menu on mobile
- Stacked layouts for two-column sections
- Single-column service cards on mobile
